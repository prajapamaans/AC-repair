## Internal Linking Strategy

### Rules
1. **Home → All service pages** — each service card links to its dedicated page
2. **Every service page → Home + Contact + 3 related services** — cross-link in body and sidebar
3. **About page → Services + Contact** — trust flow
4. **Contact page → Services + Home** — bounce reduction
5. **Footer** — links to all service pages + area keywords (text links, not images)

### Anchor Text Guidelines
- Use keyword-rich anchors: "AC repair in Vadodara", "foam wash service"
- Do NOT use generic anchors: "click here", "read more", "this page"
- Vary anchor text slightly to avoid over-optimization
