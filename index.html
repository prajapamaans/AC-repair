Frequently Asked Questions — AC Repair Vadodara

Get answers to common questions about air conditioner servicing and troubleshooting.

How much does AC repair cost in Vadodara?

Our basic AC diagnostic and inspection visit starts from only ₹299. Once the technician identifies the specific fault (e.g. capacitor, relay, sensor, PCB, or refrigerant leak), they will provide an exact itemized quotation before performing the repair. No hidden fees.

Do you offer same-day AC repair service?

Yes! We offer prompt same-day doorstep service across all parts of Vadodara. In most cases, our technician will arrive at your location within 60 to 90 minutes of receiving your booking.

Which AC brands do you service?

We service and repair all major national and international AC brands, including LG, Daikin, Voltas, Blue Star, Hitachi, Samsung, Panasonic, Carrier, Lloyd, and Whirlpool, including all modern 3-star/5-star inverter models.

Do you repair both split and window ACs?

Yes. Our certified technicians have comprehensive experience troubleshooting, servicing, dismantling, and repairing both standard high-wall Split Air Conditioners and heavy-duty window-mounted AC units.

What is AC AMC and how does it work?

An Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) is a yearly plan that covers scheduled seasonal cleanings, regular efficiency audits, priority breakdown visits, and support for your AC units. It saves money and ensures year-round peak cooling.

Do you provide AC gas filling service in Vadodara?

Yes, we provide professional leak detection, seal repair, nitrogen pressure testing, and complete gas charging (refrigerant filling) for R32, R410, and R22 gases to restore cold air delivery immediately.
