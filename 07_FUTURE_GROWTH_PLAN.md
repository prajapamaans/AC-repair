## Link Building Strategy (Ongoing)

### Free local citations (submit to these):
1. Google Business Profile ← Most important
2. JustDial — add business listing
3. Sulekha — add business listing
4. IndiaMART — create profile
5. UrbanClap / Urban Company ← competitor but worth a citation
6. TradeIndia
7. Yellow Pages India
8. Foursquare
9. Bing Places

### NAP Consistency Rule:
Name, Address, Phone must be **identical** on ALL directories.
```
Name:    Jyoti AC Repair & Service
Phone:   076989 18030
City:    Vadodara, Gujarat
Website: https://jyotiacrepair.com
```
Any variation confuses Google and hurts local rankings.
