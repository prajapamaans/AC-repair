Precision Doorstep AC Refrigerant Recharge

Simply refilling gas into a leaking system is a temporary fix; the gas will escape again within weeks, wasting money. At Jyoti AC Repair & Service, we provide a professional AC Gas Filling and leak repair program. Our certified cooling technicians use advanced nitrogen pressure testing to locate and seal micro-leaks permanently.

Once the leaks are welded using precision copper brazing torches, we perform a deep electronic vacuum process on the line to extract air and moisture. We then charge the system with premium-grade, pure refrigerant (R32, R410, or R22) based on precise factory weight parameters.

Our Complete Leak Seal & Gas Filling Process

We ensure a long-lasting fix using a rigorous 4-step workflow:

1

Nitrogen Pressure Leak Detection

We inject high-pressure nitrogen into the copper lines and evaporator coils, using soap bubble audits or electronic detectors to locate the exact leak.

2

Copper Brazing & Weld Repair

Using portable gas welding torches, the technician welds the cracked joint or copper tube, creating a secure, leak-proof seal.

3

Deep Vacuum Processing

We connect a high-power vacuum pump to pull all remaining air, nitrogen, and moisture from the lines, preparing them for the refrigerant.

4

Precision Weight Scale Charging

Using digital scales and manifold gauges, we charge the exact weight of fresh, pure R32, R410, or R22 gas, verifying peak operating pressures.

Signs Your AC is Low on Gas

  • AC blowing warm air or barely cooling
  • Thick ice/frost forming on the indoor coil fins
  • White ice building on the outdoor copper valves
  • Hissing or whistling sound along the piping
  • Compressor running constantly without cooling

Supported Gases

  • R32 — Modern eco-friendly gas
  • R410A — Inverter split AC gas
  • R22 — Standard older AC gas

FAQs — AC Gas Filling in Vadodara

Get instant answers regarding gas leak testing, copper welding, and refrigerant refilling.

Why does my AC require gas refilling? Does it consume gas?

An air conditioner operates in a sealed loop and does not consume refrigerant gas during cooling. A drop in gas levels is always caused by a physical micro-leak in the copper joints, valves, or coils. Simply topping up gas without repairing the leak is a waste of money, as the gas will leak out again.

Do you perform leak detection before filling gas?

Yes, absolutely. At Jyoti AC, we perform a thorough nitrogen pressure test to isolate the leaks. Once found, we weld the leaks with copper brazing torches, carry out vacuuming, and then perform the precision gas filling. This ensures a long-lasting fix.

Which refrigerant gas do you use?

We use only high-grade, 100% pure, non-diluted refrigerant gas matching your AC's original factory specification. This includes R32 for modern inverter units, R410A for standard split systems, and R22 for older models.

How long does the leak welding and gas filling process take?

A comprehensive service that includes nitrogen pressure leak scanning, copper joint brazing, deep vacuum extraction, and digital gas charging takes approximately 1.5 to 2.5 hours of dedicated doorstep work.
