FAQs — Split AC Repair in Vadodara

Get instant answers regarding split and inverter air conditioner diagnostics.

Why is water leaking down the wall from my Split AC?

Water leaking from an indoor split AC is almost always caused by a blocked or choked condensate drain pipe. Over time, algae, dirt, and slime accumulate inside the thin plastic drain tube, preventing water from flowing outdoors. Our technician can clear this blockage completely using high-pressure air/water flushes within 20 minutes.

What do blinking error codes on my Inverter AC mean?

Modern smart Inverter ACs feature self-diagnostic micro-controllers. When a component (like a sensor, communication cable, fan motor, or compressor module) fails, the indoor display blinks specific patterns (e.g. E1, E6, CH05). Our certified engineers carry factory diagnostic code keys to read and resolve these errors on-site.

How is a Split AC repaired compared to a Window AC?

Split AC units utilize separate indoor and outdoor units, requiring complex inter-unit wiring, copper refrigerant piping, and communication circuit boards. Window ACs are self-contained in a single chassis, which is mechanically simpler but requires removal from the window frame for major repairs.

Do you carry replacement remote controllers?

Yes! If your split AC is unresponsive due to a failed remote controller, our technician can test your system with professional universal testing remotes. We stock and supply original and premium multi-brand universal remote controllers on-site.
