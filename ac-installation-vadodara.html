Professional Doorstep AC Installation & Dismantling

Amateur AC mounting often leads to unbalanced units, loose bracket structural setups, copper pipe kinks, or uninsulated joints. This restricts air flow, causes early refrigerant leaks, and can even drop heavy units. At **Jyoti AC Repair & Service**, we provide certified **Split AC and Window AC installation and dismantling** services that conform to manufacturer safety standards.

Our structural engineers check wall densities, select heavy-duty rust-proof brackets, wrap copper lines in premium insulation tape, and perform electronic vacuuming on lines before gas commissioning. We handle all major inverter systems and standard high-tonnage units.

Our Meticulous Installation Process

We ensure a durable and high-performing setup through a structured workflow:

1

Precision Mounting & Alignment

We audit the wall space, measure perfect structural balance, and mount high-tensile brackets to prevent vibration noise and secure the unit.

2

Insulated Pipe Connection Routing

We connect the copper lines with leak-proof flare nuts, wrap them in premium weather-proof insulation sleeves, and route them to the outdoors.

3

Outdoor Condenser Positioning

We position the heavy outdoor compressor on high-density rubber pads or wall stands to secure it against strong winds and severe weather.

4

Vacuuming & Startup Commissioning

Our engineers perform vacuuming to extract moisture and air from piping before charging gas, verifying optimal temperature delivery.

FAQs — AC Installation in Vadodara

Get instant answers regarding split and window AC mounting procedures.

Are mounting brackets and copper piping included in the price?

Our standard installation labor charge covers mounting, routing, electrical connection, and testing. Any necessary physical accessories, such as heavy-duty outer wall brackets, additional copper piping (beyond the default factory kit), core drilling sleeve pipes, and drain pipes, are charged extra based on actual usage.

What is included in a Split AC re-installation during relocation?

During relocation, we provide complete **dismantling** (pump-down gas storage inside the compressor, wire disconnecting, bracket unmounting) at your old location, plus full **mounting and installation** at your new doorstep. This ensures zero refrigerant loss and maximum safety.

Why is vacuuming critical before installing a new AC?

Vacuuming utilizes an electronic pump to extract all trace air and moisture from inside the copper lines. If moisture is left in the pipes, it mixes with compressor oil to form acids, leading to compressor failure and reduced cooling. Our team vacuums every setup before startup.

How long does a standard split AC installation take?

A standard high-wall Split AC installation, including wall mounting, core drilling, balcony stand routing, and startup testing, takes approximately **1.5 to 2.5 hours** of dedicated doorstep work. Window AC setup takes around 45 to 60 minutes.
