Worry-Free Annual Cooling Care

Under peak summers, air conditioners run continuously for 12 to 18 hours per day, leading to heavy thermal loads, electrical relay stress, and rapid dust accumulation on indoor coils. At **Jyoti AC Repair & Service**, our comprehensive **Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)** are designed to keep your systems operating at peak cooling efficiency year-round.

Our standard AMC plans cover complete seasonal wet servicing, electrical capacitor and motor status audits, operating gas pressure checkups, and priority breakdown callout routing at zero auxiliary labor fees. It is the absolute cheapest way to maximize your AC’s lifespan and prevent expensive emergency breakdowns.

What is Covered in Our AC AMC

Our premium annual plans provide complete peace of mind through a robust maintenance layout:

1

2 Scheduled Seasonal Jet Cleanings

Regular high-pressure jet pump cleanings of both indoor evaporator and outdoor condenser coils to ensure clean air and maximum heat exchange.

2

Unlimited Priority Breakdown Calls

If your AC stops cooling at any point during the year, our doorstep engineer arrives within 2-4 hours. Labor visits are 100% free.

3

Electrical & Structural Health Audits

Comprehensive testing of thermistors, electrical contactors, capacitor ratings, and structural mounting bracket integrity to prevent malfunctions.

4

Gas Pressure Audits

Seasonal verification of R32, R410, or R22 refrigerant pressures. Catching slow gas micro-leaks early prevents compressor thermal burnouts.

FAQs — AC AMC in Vadodara

Get instant answers regarding our Annual Maintenance Contract plans and services.

What is included in a standard AC AMC?

Our standard Annual Maintenance Contract (Non-Comprehensive) includes 2 scheduled deep high-pressure jet wash cleanings per year, unlimited priority doorstep breakdown emergency visits with zero visit or labor charges, electrical capacitor/motor load checks, and cooling efficiency diagnostics.

What is the difference between Comprehensive and Non-Comprehensive AMC?

A **Non-Comprehensive AMC** covers all servicing, priority breakdown visits, and labor costs, but any replacement spare parts (such as fan motors, PCBs, or compressors) and refrigerant gas charging are charged extra. A **Comprehensive AMC** covers all labor, services, plus replacement spares and gas charging.

Is an AMC plan worth it for a single home AC?

Yes, absolutely. A single emergency breakdown doorstep visit plus 2 regular separate seasonal jet cleanings can easily cost more than the price of a standard annual AMC plan. Having an AMC ensures priority scheduling during hot summer months and keeps your electricity consumption low.

Do you offer AMC contracts for commercial office spaces?

Yes, we actively manage corporate commercial cooling AMC contracts for schools, sever rooms, retail stores, banks, and corporate facilities across Vadodara. We offer custom-designed corporate plans based on total tonnage and equipment counts.
