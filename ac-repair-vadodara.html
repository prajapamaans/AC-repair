FAQs — AC Repair in Vadodara

Get instant answers to commonly asked questions regarding AC repair and diagnostic procedures.

Why is my AC blowing warm air?

An AC blowing warm air is typically caused by three common failures: a dirty/blocked air filter restricting air exchange, a failed starter capacitor preventing the outdoor compressor from firing, or low refrigerant levels due to a slow leak. Our technician will pinpoint the exact cause within 15 minutes of doorstep arrival.

How long does a standard AC repair take?

Standard component replacements (like swapping a failed capacitor, sensor, contactor relay, or fan motor) are completed within 45 to 60 minutes. Major jobs like gas leak welding, compressor replacement, or complex PCB repairs may require 2 to 3 hours of dedicated work.

Do you provide any warranty on repairs?

Yes! All spare parts and components installed by Jyoti AC come with a solid manufacturer-backed replacement warranty. Additionally, we provide a 30-day doorstep service warranty on all labor adjustments.

Can you repair high-end modern Inverter ACs?

Absolutely. Our team is fully certified and equipped to troubleshoot high-end 3-star and 5-star modern smart Inverter Air Conditioners. We carry specialized circuit testing gear to diagnose inverter PCB error code sequences on-site.
