Premium Active Foaming AC Cleansing

Traditional water-only washes fail to dissolve complex oil coatings, cigarette tar residues, and kitchen grease films that bind environmental dust to copper coils. At **Jyoti AC Repair & Service**, we provide a professional **AC Foam Wash and sanitization** program that uses premium, non-corrosive, pH-neutral active foaming chemicals.

The foaming action works by expanding inside the micro-spaces of the evaporator cooling fins, chemically breaking down sticky grease, algae, and odor-producing mold spores. This deep wash restores original cooling heat exchange, resulting in a cleaner indoor breathing environment and highly optimized electrical power draw.

Our Premium Foam Washing Process

We execute a structured sanitization workflow right at your doorstep:

1

Sleeve Guard Setup

We wrap the entire indoor unit with a specialized plastic drainage sleeve, collecting all chemical wash runoff securely into a dedicated bucket.

2

Active Chemical Foam Application

We spray non-acidic active foaming agents deep into the evaporator coils and internal fan rotary drum, leaving it to expand and dissolve grime for 15 minutes.

3

Jet Pressure Rinsing

Using digital water jet pumps, we rinse away the dissolved dirt, grease, and foaming chemicals, flushing the entire drain line clear of algae blocks.

4

Coil Sanitizing & Air Audit

We apply a post-wash antibacterial sanitizer shield, dry the unit with compressed air, and audit airflow throwing speed and outlet cooling temperatures.

FAQs — AC Foam Wash in Vadodara

Get instant answers regarding active chemical foaming deep cleanings.

How is a Foam Wash different from a standard AC Jet service?

A standard Jet service uses only pressurized water, which works well for loose dust. However, a **Foam Wash** applies specialized eco-friendly chemical foaming agents. The foam expands inside the tight gaps of the cooling fins, dissolving stubborn oils, grease coatings, tar residues, and sanitizing deep-seated mold and odor-causing bacteria.

Are the chemicals safe for my family and the AC unit?

Yes, absolutely. At Jyoti AC, we use only high-end, **non-corrosive, pH-neutral, and eco-friendly** foaming chemicals. They are designed to dissolve organic grease and mold without attacking the aluminum fins or copper tubes of your AC coil, leaving behind a clean and pleasant aroma.

How long does the entire foam cleaning process take?

A deep active Foam Wash requires a bit more time than standard cleaning to allow the chemical expanding agent to dissolve the dirt. It typically takes **45 to 65 minutes** of dedicated doorstep work per Split AC unit.

Will this service eliminate musty AC smells completely?

Yes. Musty smells are caused by mold spores and bacteria thriving in the damp environment inside your AC. The antibacterial foaming chemicals strip away 99.9% of these spores and organic accumulations, instantly replacing foul odors with fresh, sanitized air.
